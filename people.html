Jonathan Gratch
Principal Investigator · Professor

Professor at Vanderbilt University's College of Connected Computing. His research focuses on the interplay of emotion, social behavior, and intelligent systems — developing computational models that underpin virtual humans capable of emotionally intelligent interaction.
